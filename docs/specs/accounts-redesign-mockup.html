Concept A · The Ledger

spreadsheet · keyboard-first · highest density

Full-width sortable table with category bands. Arrow keys move the selection, Enter opens a right drawer to edit. Drag the grip handle to reorder; drop a row onto a category band to reparent. KPI strip is demoted to a sticky footer ribbon.

Concept B · The Pinboard

kanban · card-based · drag-heavy

Categories are vertical stacks; accounts are draggable cards. Card colour comes from the category, not the kind, so the board reads at a glance. Owner becomes a small pill, not a grouping axis. Click any card → side drawer (same as concept A).

Concept C · The Atlas recommended

master-detail · treemap · scales to 20+ categories

Two-pane layout. Left rail = portfolio treemap (rectangle area = % of net worth) plus a draggable outline of categories and accounts. Right pane = roomy edit surface for whichever row/category you select. The treemap turns "where's my money concentrated?" into a 0.5-second glance instead of mental arithmetic on totals.
